Mental Illness Q & A

What is a mental illness?

A mental illness is a variety of mental health disorders/conditions that can affect your mood, the way you think, and your behavior.
Examples include: Depression, Anxiety, Bi-Polar Disorder

What are some signs/symptoms that you might have a mental illness?

Symptoms include prolonged sadness, extreme mood changes, withdrawing yourself from social activities, excessive worry, changes in your sleeping and eating habits, difficultly concentrating

How common is a mental illness?

Mental Ilnesses are fairly common, about 1 in 5 adults experience some form of a mental health illness

What causes mental illnesses?

Mental Ilnesses can be caused by a mix of genetic, biological, and environmental factors.

How can I help someone with a mental illness?

You can support that person by listening to them and NOT judging them for their mental illness, it is not something they can control. Encourage them to seek private or group therapy with a bunch of people with that mental illness. You can also offer that person emotional support

Is there a stigma (negative belief) when it comes to mental illnesses?

Yes there is a stigma surronding mental health but it is starting to decrease as awareness is increasing about mental illnesses around the world.
